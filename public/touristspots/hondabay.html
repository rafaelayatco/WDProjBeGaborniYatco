Honda Bay

Honda Bay is a popular destination in Puerto Princesa known for its white-sand beaches, beautiful island scenery, and colorful coral reefs. Visitors can enjoy snorkeling, diving, and island hopping. The bay's name comes from the Spanish term Bahía Honda, meaning “deep bay,” and locals pronounce the “H” silently.

Location: Eastern coast of Palawan, near Puerto Princesa City.

Best Time to Visit: November to May (dry season).
