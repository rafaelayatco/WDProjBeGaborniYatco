Tourist Spots

Welcome to our interactive guide to the Philippines, an archipelago of over 7,000 islands where breathtaking natural wonders meet a rich, multi-layered history. From the emerald-green Banaue Rice Terraces in the north to the crystal-clear lagoons of Palawan and the world-famous white sands of Boracay, the Philippines offers a diverse landscape for every type of traveler. Whether you are looking to dive into the vibrant coral reefs of the Tubbataha Reef, trek through lush tropical jungles with hiking trips, or explore and do island hopping, there is an unforgettable experience waiting around every corner.

Our interactive map is designed to help you navigate this tropical paradise with ease. Click on any red dot on the map to explore some of the Philippines’ most iconic tourist destinations. Each location features a curated look at must-see landmarks, local activities, and with travel tips to help you plan in the other tabs for your perfect island-hopping adventure. Dive in and discover why millions of travelers fall in love with the warmth, beauty, and "endless summer" of the Philippines.
