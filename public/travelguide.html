Travel Guide

Budget & Planning

  • Check for ATMs on the islands in the Philippines.
  • Keep pesos at hand for airport departure fees.
  • If you’re low on budget or time, book everything ahead.
  • Leave plenty of time for travelling between islands.
  • Get proof of any delays or cancellations immediately.

Safety & Health

  • Do not drink tap water in the Philippines.
  • Take mosquito repellent at all times.
  • Bring all medication you might need.
  • Invest in a waterproof diving bag.

Packing Essentials

  • Pack lightly.
  • Take flip flops and expect to ruin them.
  • Bring comfortable water shoes.
  • Take a jumper, leggings, and socks for cold transport.
  • Use an umbrella instead of a raincoat.

Local Experience

  • Befriend the Filipino locals — they’re warm and helpful.
  • Buy a local SIM or portable WiFi hotspot.
  • Always try to get a window seat when travelling.
